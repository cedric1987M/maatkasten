# Competitor Pricing Analysis

## Maatkasten Online (Belgium)
- **Open dressing**: €350/linear meter
- **Closed dressing (with doors)**: €470/linear meter
- **Includes**: VAT + Home delivery
- **Installation**: DIY (no installation cost)
- **Quality**: 18mm thick panels, 8mm cabinet backs, soft-close drawers
- **Advantage**: 2-3x cheaper than traditional carpenters

### Example Calculation (200cm wide wardrobe):
- Open: 200cm × €350 = €700
- Closed: 200cm × €470 = €940

## Market Positioning
- Maatkasten Online focuses on **affordability** (DIY assembly)
- Premium options: Wood textures, sloped roofs, drawers
- Simple designs are cheapest
- Doors add 30-35% to cost

## Our Current Pricing
- Wardrobe (200×220×60): €1,137 (with 40% margin)
- Cost: €682

### Analysis
- Our pricing is **HIGHER** than Maatkasten Online per linear meter
- Maatkasten: €470/m for 200cm = €940 (closed)
- Us: €1,137 for 200×220×60 = €1,137

**ISSUE**: We're pricing at €5.68/cm² vs Maatkasten at €4.70/cm²

## Recommendation
- Adjust pricing to be more competitive
- Consider €800-950 range for standard wardrobe
- Keep 40% margin but reduce material cost assumptions
- OR: Highlight superior quality/service to justify premium pricing
